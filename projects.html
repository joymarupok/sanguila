Instagram is a photo and video sharing app that enables users to capture moments, apply filters, and share them on their profiles or with their followers. It was launched in 2010 and later acquired by Facebook, becoming one of the most popular social media platforms globally.

Twitter

Twitter is a microblogging platform that allows users to post and interact with short messages known as tweets. Users can follow others, like and retweet tweets, and participate in trending discussions. Twitter was founded in 2006 and has become a prominent platform for real-time news and social networking.
